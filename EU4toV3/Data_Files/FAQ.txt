A: You probably Enabled the mod in a playset that isn't active.

Q: How can I customize my conversion before converting?
Scan through configurables/ folder and look into the myriad of configuration files - a great majority of these files is intended to be modified by users to achieve specific conversion results.
There are also options in the frontend configuration menu - these are toggles most commonly used and with greatest conversion impact.

Q: How can I customize my game after converting?
A: Use the guide in after_converting.txt in the converter subdirectory.
